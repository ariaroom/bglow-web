03 · Founder's Story

B-Glow began with a question.

While pursuing her Master of Music in Classical Voice at Manhattan School of Music, Soohyun Aria Jo constantly asked herself:

"What kind of positive influence can I, as an individual, offer to others in this world?"

Her answer was Art. Art is a space where the inherent sparkle within each of us is revealed and empowered. This brilliance is not an external force but an internal energy: the essence of one's true self. By encouraging constant self-observation and understanding, art helps release suppressed emotions. Through the mirror of art, audiences can finally set aside their social masks and begin to breathe as their authentic selves.

Through the window of art, she hopes people find hope, consolation, and heartfelt encouragement. In a world where our individual light is often dimmed by the frantic pace of life, B-Glow strives to help everyone rediscover their own glow and live according to their unique nature. We are here to help people discover their potential and cultivate the strength to live a more meaningful life.

"I want to help people rediscover the light in their own eyes, and feel the true value of this life."

Soohyun Aria Jo, Founder
