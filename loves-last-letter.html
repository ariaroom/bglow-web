Nature

Nature & Resilience (The Inherent Light)

Digital photography and video capturing the emerald sea and "Yoonseul" (shimmering reflection of sunlight on water) symbolize the world's inherent beauty.

Just like nature shines in its pure state, this work shows that no matter how difficult life becomes, our innate brilliance and value never fade and remains unchanged.

The majesty of nature acts as a catalyst to rebuild our will to breathe and move forward, proving that, even in the darkest moments, our value is never lost.

Dog

Unconditional Love (An Expanding Universe)

This piece explores the infinite expansion of love and the profound comfort found in a companion animal. A pet is living proof of how our inner worlds broaden as the circle of those we love grows. The shift in perspective finding joy in the small, fleeting lives around us is the purest transformation of worldview taught to us by these companions.

The work pays tribute to a being who conveyed a wholeness of love through a silent, deep gaze. Their devotion, where their entire world centered on "me," and the human regret of not fully grasping that heart, are sublimated into a solemn promise of eternal reunion.

Ultimately, a companion is more than a pet; they are mentors of pure love and a gateway to the world’s hidden beauty. The bond they leave behind sustains the soul, remaining as an inextinguishable warmth within our gaze.

Lover

Love & Connection (Sanctuary and Growth)

A depiction of a couple laughing and locking eyes amidst a heavy downpour reveals that true love is being happy as long as you are together, even in the middle of a storm.

Standing before the purity of love, we finally shed our social masks to rediscover our most innocent selves. This profound immersion allows us to realize who we truly are and provides a sacred space for spiritual growth, proving that the strength to endure any "storm" is already ignited by the presence of another.

Mom

The Mother (Inheritance & Independence)

This collection of painting and photography explores the inheritance of love and the journey toward independence. The mother, composed of purples, blues, and mints, reflects her own history and unique identity. The child, primarily in vibrant yellows and reds, carries strokes of the mother's colors, symbolizing the profound influence and genetic bond they share.

Above them, white star-looking fragments are actually falling stones that represents life’s hardships. While the mother once shielded the child from these stones, the painting captures the inevitable nature when the child, now grown, must leave that embrace. It is a poignant reminder that every individual must eventually face the world's challenges with their own strength, carrying the light they inherited into their own dazzling future

Myself

The Final Awakening (Interactive Performance)

The performance video captures the ultimate realization: that the person we love most is, in fact, ourselves. Our past struggles were born from a deep desire to be happy and a profound love for our own existence. In this installation, the performance begins only when an audience member places a white flower into a vase a symbolic ritual of honoring one’s life and awakening their suppressed inner song.

This interaction transforms the "last letter" into a courageous declaration of self-forgiveness and self-love. Ultimately, this work proves the core mission of B-Glow: that every life is already luminous, and every individual possesses an innate brilliance waiting to be rediscovered.

Interactive Instruction: The song begins only when an audience member places a white flower into the vase.
